On this page
Test Framework has provided a simple and straightforward tests center where codec developers could easily trigger a test with a simple click or scheduled task.
Through these tests, a developer could easily simplify there procedure of continuous integration as well as improve the quality of their codecs.
Developers could easily get to know the quality of a deployed codebase through conformance tests with more details to come in performance test , while a coverage test could inform the developers that to which extent the codec has been covered by the unit tests' suit deployed with Test Framework.
Test Framework's test centre

① return to test centre, when navigating on a specific test page, you may click on the logo to return to the test centre.

③ Entrance to job centre,this mark will only show up when thre is a few jobs are running or being scheduled, you may click it to open the job centre.
